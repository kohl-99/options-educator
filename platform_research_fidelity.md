# Fidelity Platform Research

## Options Trading Tiers and Supported Strategies

Fidelity offers **3 tiers** of options trading permissions.

### Tier 1 (Basic Options)
**Strategies Included:**
- Buy-writes
- Selling covered calls
- Rolling covered calls
- Buying calls/puts
- Selling cash covered puts
- Long straddles/strangles

**Description:** Entry-level options trading focused on basic strategies with limited risk. Suitable for beginners who want to learn options trading fundamentals.

**Requirements:**
- Available on standard brokerage accounts
- No margin requirement

### Tier 2 (Intermediate Spreads)
**Strategies Included (Everything in Tier 1 plus):**
- Buy-writes
- Selling covered calls
- Rolling covered calls
- Buying calls/puts
- Selling cash covered puts
- Long straddles/strangles
- Spreads (up to 4 legs)
- Selling covered puts-short stock secured

**Description:** Intermediate level allowing spread strategies with defined risk. Requires understanding of multi-leg options strategies.

**Requirements:**
- Margin account required
- More trading experience needed
- Higher financial requirements

### Tier 3 (Advanced Strategies)
**Strategies Included (Everything in Tier 1 & 2 plus):**
- Buy-writes
- Selling covered calls
- Rolling covered calls
- Buying calls/puts
- Selling cash covered puts
- Long straddles/strangles
- Spreads (up to 4 legs)
- Selling covered puts-short stock secured
- **For stocks, ETFs, and Indexes:**
  - Selling uncovered calls/puts
  - Short straddles

**Description:** Most advanced tier allowing naked options and short straddles. Highest risk strategies with unlimited loss potential.

**Requirements:**
- Margin account required
- Significant trading experience
- Higher net worth and income requirements
- Understanding of unlimited risk strategies

## Platform Features

### Options Strategy Tools
- Multi-leg strategy builder
- Profit/loss calculators
- Risk analysis tools
- Greeks display

### Trading Platforms
- **Fidelity.com Web Platform** - Full-featured browser-based trading
- **Fidelity Trader+ Web** - Advanced active trader platform
- **Active Trader Pro** - Desktop application for professional traders
- **Fidelity Mobile App** - Full options trading on mobile

### Educational Resources
- Options education center
- Strategy guides
- Video tutorials
- Weekly "In the Money" video series with trade ideas

## Supported Products
- Stock options
- ETF options
- Index options

## Key Advantages
1. **Three-Tier System** - More granular progression than Robinhood (2 levels)
2. **Advanced Strategies** - Allows naked options at Tier 3
3. **Professional Tools** - Active Trader Pro and Trader+ platforms
4. **Educational Support** - Comprehensive learning resources
5. **IRA Options Trading** - Tiers 1 and 2 available in IRAs

## Limitations
1. **Margin Requirements** - Tiers 2 and 3 require margin accounts
2. **Approval Process** - More rigorous than beginner-focused platforms
3. **Commission Structure** - $0.65 per contract (plus regulatory fees)
4. **Complex Interface** - Steeper learning curve than simplified platforms

## IRA Restrictions
- Inherited IRA (BDA): Option Levels 1 or 2 only
- Spreads trading in Fidelity IRAs requires Option Level 2 approval
- No naked options in IRAs

## Important Notes
- Options trading strategies involve varying degrees of risk and complexity
- Not all strategies are suitable for all investors
- Approval requirements are more rigorous at subsequent tiers
- Financial situation, trading experience, and investment objectives are considered for approval
- Additional costs associated with multi-leg strategies (spreads, straddles, collars)
