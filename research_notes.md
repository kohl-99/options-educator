# Options Trading Strategies Research Notes

## Complete List of Options Trading Strategies

### Rookies (Beginner Level)
1. **Covered Call** - Selling calls against owned stock
2. **Protective Put** - Buying puts to protect owned stock
3. **Collar** - Combination of protective put and covered call
4. **Cash-Secured Put** - Selling puts with cash reserved to buy stock

### Veterans (Intermediate Level)
5. **Long Call** - Buying call options for bullish outlook
6. **Long Put** - Buying put options for bearish outlook
7. **Fig Leaf** - LEAPS call with short call
8. **Long Call Spread (Bull Call Spread)** - Buy lower strike call, sell higher strike call
9. **Long Put Spread (Bear Put Spread)** - Buy higher strike put, sell lower strike put

### Seasoned Veterans (Advanced Level)
10. **Short Call Spread (Bear Call Spread)** - Sell lower strike call, buy higher strike call
11. **Short Put Spread (Bull Put Spread)** - Sell higher strike put, buy lower strike put
12. **Long Straddle** - Buy call and put at same strike
13. **Long Strangle** - Buy call and put at different strikes
14. **Back Spread with Calls** - Ratio spread favoring long calls
15. **Back Spread with Puts** - Ratio spread favoring long puts
16. **Long Calendar Spread with Calls** - Sell near-term call, buy longer-term call
17. **Long Calendar Spread with Puts** - Sell near-term put, buy longer-term put
18. **Diagonal Spread with Calls** - Calendar spread with different strikes
19. **Diagonal Spread with Puts** - Calendar spread with different strikes
20. **Long Butterfly with Calls** - Three-strike strategy with calls
21. **Long Butterfly with Puts** - Three-strike strategy with puts
22. **Iron Butterfly** - Combination of short put and call spreads
23. **Skip Strike Butterfly with Calls** - Modified butterfly with skipped strike
24. **Skip Strike Butterfly with Puts** - Modified butterfly with skipped strike
25. **Inverse Skip Strike Butterfly with Calls** - Reverse skip strike structure
26. **Inverse Skip Strike Butterfly with Puts** - Reverse skip strike structure
27. **Christmas Tree Butterfly with Calls** - Asymmetric butterfly with calls
28. **Christmas Tree Butterfly with Puts** - Asymmetric butterfly with puts
29. **Long Condor Spread with Calls** - Four-strike strategy with calls
30. **Long Condor Spread with Puts** - Four-strike strategy with puts
31. **Iron Condor** - Combination of short put and call spreads at different strikes

### All-Stars (Expert Level)
32. **Short Call** - Selling naked calls
33. **Short Put** - Selling naked puts
34. **Short Straddle** - Sell call and put at same strike
35. **Short Strangle** - Sell call and put at different strikes
36. **Long Combination (Synthetic Long Stock)** - Buy call and sell put at same strike
37. **Short Combination (Synthetic Short Stock)** - Buy put and sell call at same strike
38. **Front Spread with Calls** - Ratio spread favoring short calls
39. **Front Spread with Puts** - Ratio spread favoring short puts
40. **Double Diagonal** - Two diagonal spreads combined
