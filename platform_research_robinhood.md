# Robinhood Platform Research

## Options Trading Levels and Supported Strategies

Robinhood offers **2 levels** of options trading permissions (Level 2 and Level 3).

### Level 2 (Basic Options Strategies)
**Strategies Included:**
- Long Call (Buy calls)
- Long Put (Buy puts)
- Covered Call (Sell calls against owned stock)
- Cash-Secured Put (Sell puts with cash collateral)

**Description:** Basic level for beginners. Allows buying options and selling covered positions. Limited risk strategies only.

**Requirements:**
- Available on Robinhood Instant and Gold accounts
- Not available on Cash accounts

### Level 3 (Advanced Options Strategies)
**Strategies Included (Everything in Level 2 plus):**
- Credit Spreads (Bull Put Spread, Bear Call Spread)
- Debit Spreads (Bull Call Spread, Bear Put Spread)
- Iron Condor
- Iron Butterfly
- Straddles
- Strangles
- Calendar Spreads
- Vertical Spreads

**Description:** Advanced multi-leg strategies with defined risk. Suitable for experienced traders.

**Requirements:**
- Available on Robinhood Instant and Gold accounts
- Requires more trading experience and knowledge
- Not available on Cash accounts

## Platform Features

### Options Strategy Builder
- Interactive tool for building and customizing strategies
- Visual profit/loss charts
- Helps users learn about different strategies
- Supports both basic and advanced strategies

### Simulated Returns
- Shows potential outcomes before placing trades
- Helps visualize profit and loss scenarios

### Profit and Loss Charts
- Real-time P&L visualization
- Shows breakeven points
- Displays maximum profit and loss

### Mobile-First Experience
- Full options trading on mobile app
- User-friendly interface
- Quick order entry

## Supported Products
- Stock options
- ETF options
- Index options

## Key Advantages
1. **Commission-Free Trading** - No fees for options trades (only regulatory fees)
2. **User-Friendly Interface** - Simplified for beginners
3. **Strategy Builder** - Interactive learning tool
4. **Mobile Experience** - Full-featured mobile app
5. **Low Barrier to Entry** - Easy account setup

## Limitations
1. **Only 2 Levels** - Less granular than other platforms (vs 4 levels on IBKR)
2. **No Naked Options** - Cannot sell uncovered calls or puts
3. **Limited Advanced Strategies** - No complex butterflies or condors beyond iron versions
4. **Account Type Restrictions** - Options not available on Cash accounts
