# Interactive Brokers Platform Research

## Options Trading Levels and Supported Strategies

Interactive Brokers offers **4 levels** of options trading permissions, each unlocking progressively more advanced strategies.

### Level 1 (Beginner)
**Strategies Included:**
- Covered Call / Covered Basket Call
- Buy Write (buying stock and selling call simultaneously)

**Description:** Most conservative level, only allows strategies with owned stock as collateral.

### Level 2 (Intermediate)
**Strategies Included (Everything in Level 1 plus):**
- Long Call
- Long Put
- Covered Put
- Protective Call
- Protective Put
- Long Straddle
- Long Strangle
- Conversion
- Long Call Spread
- Long Put Spread
- Long Iron Condor
- Long Box Spread
- Collar
- Short Collar

**Description:** Adds basic long options and defined-risk spreads. Suitable for traders with basic options knowledge.

### Level 3 (Advanced)
**Strategies Included (Everything in Levels 1 & 2 plus):**
- Short Put
- Synthetic
- Reversal
- Short Call Spread
- Short Put Spread
- Short Iron Condor
- Long Butterfly
- Unbalanced Butterfly
- Short Butterfly
- Calendar Spread - Debit
- Diagonal Spread - Short leg expires first

**Description:** Adds credit spreads and more complex multi-leg strategies. Requires margin account.

### Level 4 (Expert)
**Strategies Included (Everything in Levels 1, 2 & 3 plus):**
- Short Naked Call
- Short Straddle
- Short Strangle
- Short Synthetic
- Calendar Spread - Credit
- Diagonal Spread - Long leg expires first

**Description:** Highest level with unlimited risk strategies like naked calls. Requires significant experience and capital.

## Platform Features

### Strategy Builder
- Create, analyze, and trade multi-leg options strategies
- Supports spreads, condors, straddles, and more
- Available in TWS (Trader Workstation) and web platform

### Option Strategy Lab
- Comprehensive trading tool
- Helps identify and analyze options strategies based on market forecasts
- Available in TWS

### Options Strategies Web App
- Free tool for viewing profit-and-loss profiles
- Option combinations analysis

### OptionTrader
- Multiple option chains in tabs
- Extensive data points
- Advanced order entry

### Mobile Options Trading
- Full options trading capabilities on mobile devices
- Access to 30+ marketplaces
